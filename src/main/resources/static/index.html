About Our School

Our school focuses on providing excellent education while fostering a nurturing environment for students' holistic development. With experienced faculty, modern teaching methods and strong academic values, we ensure that every student receives the support and guidance needed to excel academically and personally. ⭐ Rated 4.3 by parents and students for quality education.

Curriculum & Medium of Instruction

At Glory English Medium School in Mangaldas Nagar, Guntur, we provide a well-rounded educational experience that promotes academic excellence along with overall personality development. Our curriculum includes a balanced combination of academic subjects, arts, co-curricular activities, and physical education to help students develop knowledge, creativity, discipline, and essential life skills.
